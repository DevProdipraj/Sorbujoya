Frequently Asked Questions

Shorbojoya is an award-winning agri-fintech business that enables small-scale farmers and Agri Businesses to maximize their profit. iFarmer bundles everything that a farmer needs: Farm financing, agriculture inputs, advisory, insurance and access to markets.

Shorbojoya empowers farmers and small/micro agri entrepreneurs by providing access to capital through connecting them with farm financing, training on best farming techniques, sales of farm produce to boost the quality and quantity of their farm production and also provide a healthy earning after harvest for both farmer and funder.

We are a team of passionate men and women, who have years of experience in the Agriculture, Finance and Technology sector. Our common goal is to find ways to create a positive impact on rural farmers, contribute to the growth of food production, improve food security in Bangladesh, and to ensure more contribution in the Agriculture sector to enable us to feed the growing population of Bangladesh. Post to your signup using the iFarmer mobile application, make sure to complete your profile by filling out the necessary details. You can then check the available farms for funding and fund by following the steps as you move along the way. At the end of the stipulated farming period, you will receive a percentage of the earnings made from the sale of the produce along with your initial fund (Principal amount) in your designated bank account mentioned on your profile.

Each successful funding is carried forward in the following way: 1. Payment made through electronic fund transfers or cheque 2. Payment verified by Team iFarmer 3. Invoice is issued 4. The 5 working day refund period begins where the funder is entitled to a full refund (After deducting 2.5% transaction fee) if he/she requests for it 5. Following the end of the refund period, the fund is transferred to the field operations team for further perusal 6. 15/16 days after the date mentioned on the invoice, the certificate of ownership is issued, stating that the farm is fully operational. The Certificate of Ownership explains the ownership of the asset and iFarmer’s responsibilities in this regard. For any further assistance, please reach out to us directly by writing to us at hello@ifarmer.asia or calling our hotline number at +880 130 253 6026.

Depending on the farm you select, the cycle can run from 4 months to up to 12 months. At the end of the farm cycle iFarmer facilitates the sale of the agricultural product and gives you your earnings within the stipulated time as mentioned on the certificate.

Earning is based on a earning-sharing criterion and will depend on the farm you are buying. For example, if you are buying a cow farm with 15% projected earnings, at the end of the farm cycle you will receive a total of your principal amount plus the 15% projected earnings on your principal amount.

The initial funded amount (Principal) amount and earnings (Principal+Gain) after sales of the produce will be paid into the account details provided by you when you have set up your profile. You can edit your profile using the iFarmer mobile application to update your bank details and other information.
